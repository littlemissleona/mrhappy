Mr. Happy

Mr Happy was always happy, but on this particular day he was especially happy. Can you guess why?

It was Mr Happy's birthday!

Mr Happy decided to have a party, so he set off to invite his friends and buy the things he needed. First, he met Little Miss Busy. Good morning! Mr Happy said. Would you like to ... but Little Miss Busy was already rushing away! Sorry, can't stop! she called back.

I suppose she's too busy to come to a party anyway, said Mr Happy. I'll ask someone else.

Mr Happy went to the cake shop. Mr Happy thought it would be nice to share a birthday cake with his friends.

But Mr Greedy was already in the shop, buying the last cake. Sorry, can't chat! Mr Geedy said, licking his lips as he rushed off. He didn't say happy birthday to Mr Happy, but Mr Happy didn't mind. Not to worry, said Mr Happy. I'll go and get some balloons.

On his way to the balloon shop, Mr Happy saw Little Miss Helpful. She was carrying a lot of balloons. Hello, Little Miss Helpful! said Mr Happy. I see you've been to the ballon shop. Can you please tell me the way? he asked. Sorry, I...I can't help you! said Little Miss Helpful, and she rushed off.

She didn't wish Mr Happy a happy birthday and she didn't offer to share her balloons.That's a very odd, thought Mr Happy. Little Miss Helpful is normally SO helpful.

Just then, he spotted Mr and Little Miss Birthday, hurrying along. Mr Happy smiled. They will definitely come to my party! he thought. But...

Sorry, we've got plans today, they told him as they rushed off. Even they didn't say happy birthday - and they loved birthdays more than anyone!

Mr Happy decided to give up and go home. But can you imagine what he found when he got there? All his friends!

Mr Birthday and Little Miss Birthday were there! Little Miss Helpful was there with the balloons. Mr Greedy was there with the cake. And Little Miss Busy was there, too. She'd been busy because she was planning a suprise party for him!

Happy Birthday! they all cheered. Mr Happy grinned a very big grin. It really is a happy birthday! he chuckled.
