Mr. Happy

Mr Happy was always happy, but on this particular day he was especially happy. Can you guess why?

It was Mr Happy's birthday!

Mr Happy decided to have a party, so he set off to invite his friends and buy the things he needed. First, he met Little Miss Busy. Good morning! Mr Happy said. Would you like to ... but Little Miss Busy was already rushing away! Sorry, can't stop! she called back.

I suppose she's too busy to come to a party anyway, said Mr Happy. I'll ask someone else.

Mr Happy went to the cake shop. Mr Happy thought it would be nice to share a birthday cake with his friends.

But Mr Greedy was already in the shop, buying the last cake. Sorry, can't chat! Mr Geedy said, licking his lips as he rushed off. He didn't say happy birthday to Mr Happy, but Mr Happy didn't mind. Not to worry, said Mr Happy. I'll go and get some balloons.
